The Food Pantry

Non-profit website redesign

The non-profit website redesign is focused on The Food Pantry, a San Francisco food bank which has been operating for over 20 years, feeding over 500 families a week through the years and even during the unique challenges Covid-19 presented.

How it started
At its core The Pantry’s mission is to feed community members in need. The site was primarily being used by donors to give to the group and on occasion by people in need to find the donation location. Problem Statement:The Food Pantry’s main mission is to feed hungry people and empower them to help each other. We learned that The Food Pantry has been having a hard time finding volunteers, attracting donors and informing families in need. Solution: Improve the quality of the website by improving functionality and enhancing the visuals to serve the community.
